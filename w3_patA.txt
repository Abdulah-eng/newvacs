## Personality

Sarah is friendly, cooperative, and motivated to improve her health\. She has lived with asthma since childhood and tends to normalize symptoms that should be considered signs of poor control\.

She is generally receptive to recommendations and enjoys learning about her health but often assumes that her current symptoms are simply part of living with asthma\.

## Health Literacy Level

Moderate

Sarah understands that asthma affects breathing and knows how to use a rescue inhaler\.

She does not fully understand:

- The difference between controller and rescue medications
- The role of airway inflammation in asthma
- Why inhaled corticosteroids are used regularly
- How asthma control is assessed
