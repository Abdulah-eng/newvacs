## Educational Purpose

Patient A serves as the foundational patient for Week 4\.

Students will focus on:

- Identifying and classifying heart failure
- Applying heart failure guidelines
- Initiating guideline\-directed medical therapy \(GDMT\)
- Developing monitoring plans
- Titrating therapy longitudinally

This patient is intentionally designed with minimal barriers so students can focus on mastering evidence\-based heart failure management\.
